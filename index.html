Our Manufacturing Process

From raw materials to finished products

Agro Paper Production

01

Collection & Sorting

We source agricultural residue and city leaves from local farmers and municipalities, ensuring quality raw materials while supporting local communities.

02

Cleaning & Preparation

Raw materials undergo thorough cleaning and processing to remove impurities, preparing them for the pulping stage.

03

Pulping & Refining

Materials are converted into high-quality pulp using our efficient biomass-powered systems, creating the base for all our paper products.

04

Formation & Finishing

The refined pulp is formed into paper sheets, cups, bags, or wrapping material, then dried and finished to meet quality standards.

Cloth Bag Production

01

Fiber Collection

We collect textile offcuts from tailors and recovered fibers, preventing valuable materials from ending up in landfills.

02

Quality Sorting

Fibers are carefully sorted by type, color, and quality to ensure the best materials are selected for each product line.

03

Design & Cutting

Our design team creates patterns and cuts the fabric pieces, optimizing material usage while ensuring stylish designs.

04

Stitching & Quality Check

Skilled artisans stitch the bags with precision, followed by rigorous quality checks to ensure durability and aesthetic appeal.
